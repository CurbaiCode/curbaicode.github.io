Controls for Do-Not-Track Features

Most web browsers and some mobile operating systems and mobile applications include a Do-Not-Track (“DNT”) feature or setting you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online browsing activities monitored and collected. At this stage no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals has been finalized. However, we assume all of our users do not wish to be tracked and therefore we do not track our users or currently respond to DNT browser signals or any other mechanism that automatically communicates your choice not to be tracked online.
