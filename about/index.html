ABOUT CURBAI


Curbai  noun
Cur•bai  |  \ ˈkərˈbaɪ \

Derived from the Latin word for the melon family, Cucurbitaceae, Curbai is dedicated to producing the best applications, extensions, and other resources for every user. Those who work at Curbai believe in cultivating a comfortable and efficient electronic environment for their customers and do their best to ensure the quality of everything they grow.

We create macOS and iOS apps, command-line tools, and websites and web apps. We also specialize in 3D graphics, modeling, rendering, and printing, as well as UI/UX, icon, logo, and typeface design and creation.

OUR PARTNERS

Digital Parfait Electronics

In 2021, Curbai along with Labrium and TechPro Signature partnered to form Digital Parfait Electronics. Digital Parfait Electronics with its purpose of providing a smooth transfer of information to further the advancement of its members, is the most powerful partnership formed with Curbai.

Labrium

labrium.github.io

Labrium provides apps that make life on macOS much easier and more customizable. They specialize in macOS desktop apps, web simulations and games utilizing 3D graphics technologies, wearable technology and UI/UX and operating system design, and computational math and physics.

TechPro Signature

techprosignature.github.io

TechPro Signature is set on providing the best possible balance between high quality, time-efficient, and inexpensive solutions. They have developed and implemented communication tools that are still in use today. TechPro Signature specializes in delivering the highest performance desktop and web apps, as well as innovative technical support solutions.
